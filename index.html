What is the difference between Robux B/T and Robux A/T?
Roblox has a 30% tax on all robux gamepass purchases.
If you bought 1000 Robux B/T, you would receive 700, 1000 - 30% = 700.
If you were to buy 1000 A/T, you would receive 1000 because you will be asked to set your gamepass price to 1429, which is 1000 + 30%.
